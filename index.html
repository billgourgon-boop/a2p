About APW Flooring & Home Remodeling

APW Flooring & Home Remodeling — operated by Angier Paint, Wallpaper & Carpet Service, Inc. — has been a family-owned business in Angier, North Carolina since 1973. For more than 50 years we’ve helped homeowners across the Raleigh area with quality flooring and remodeling done the right way.

We carry all major brands of flooring and tile and provide turnkey installation, so you have one trusted, service-oriented crew from selection through completion. From hardwood, luxury vinyl plank, tile, and carpet to full home remodeling — no surprise costs, just clean work, clear communication, and floors you’ll be proud of for years to come.
